Pot Strudel

There is nothing like a flakey pastry with your morning coffee or tea! Prep this the night before or the morning of and pop into the oven about an hour before eating.

It is recommended to drizzle icing over the pastry before serving. While no recipe is provided, a simple icing of icing (powdered) sugar, melted butter, and milk can be quickly made. If not, a dusting of powdered sugar will suffice.

INGREDIENTS

For the dough:

  • 600 g flour
  • 250 g margarine
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

For the filling:

  • 500 g quark
  • 2 tbsp coffee cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • lemon zest
  • 100 g raisins
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • icing sugar

STEPS

  1. Combine flour, melted lukewarm margarine, egg, vinegar, and about 300 ml of warm salt water to prepare a dough. Work the intial sitcky dough for a while, knock and beat it every now and then with full force. Pick it up and slam it on the pastry board until it no longer sticks.
  2. Form a loaf, sprinkle with flour, cover, and let rest for about 15 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough partially on a floured cloth with a rolling pin. Then very gently stretch it out using the back of the hands. Drizzle with melted fat and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
  4. Mix the quark with the beaten egg yolks, sugar, vanilla, and lemon zest well. Add the raisins and finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
  5. Fill up the dough, roll on the sheet, brush with melted butter, and bake about 45 minutes.
  6. Cut into slices and serve with icing sugar.
